Clicking on the account users name on the header provides a drop down of options

<Frame caption="Menu shown when you click the name dropdown">
  ![Menu shown when you click the name dropdown](/images/rediq/namedropdown.png)
</Frame>

- ### **Edit Account**
  - Customize account information and colors on [Valuation Model](../../valuationIQ/how-to-use-the-model/customize-model-colors)

    ![The Edit Account screen](/images/rediq/editaccountscreenrediq.png)
- ### [**Manage Users**](../settings-and-admin/addmanage-users)
- ### [**Manage Assumptions**](../../valuationIQ/how-to-use-the-model/preset-assumptions)
  - Preset Assumptions allows redIQ valuationIQ users to save sets of assumptions that they commonly use when underwriting deals.
- ### [**Charts of Accounts**](../operating-statements/chart-of-accounts)
  - The Chart of Accounts is a list of categories that is used as line items in the Cash Flow of all Historicals.

<Warning>
  _Only corporate admins can change chart of accounts. If you wish to replace all accounts, please contact your Customer Success Manager_
</Warning>

- ### [**Bulk upload**](../operating-statements/operating-statements-and-property-data-bulk-upload)
  - redIQ offers the ability to upload property and operating statements data from your property management system directly into redIQ system by means of bulk upload.
- ### [**Change Password**](../settings-and-admin/password-recovery)
