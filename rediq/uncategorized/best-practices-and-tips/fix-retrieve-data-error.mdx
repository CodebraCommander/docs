**Clear Add-In Cache**


### For Windows Users


<Steps>
  <Step title="To begin, restart your PC. Please be sure to save all work beforehand.">
  </Step>
  <Step title="When your PC has restarted, do not open any applications, and close any that may open.">
  </Step>
  <Step title="Press and hold the Windows key, and then press and hold the R key.">
  </Step>
  <Step title="An input box will appear. Copy the following address into the input box and click “OK”:">
  </Step>
</Steps>


```
%LOCALAPPDATA%\Microsoft\Office\16.0\Wef\
```
5. Once you have navigated to the address, select all contents by pressing and holding CTRL and then pressing the A key. Then, delete all contents in the folder.
6. Once you’ve deleted the contents, open Excel. Click on the QuickSync add-in and log in to QuickSync.


### For Mac Users


<Steps>
  <Step title="To begin, click on the QuickSync add-in on the excel, then click on the little “i” button on the top right corner of the page to see the redIQ Add-in drop dropdown menu.">
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select “Clear web cache” from the dropdown menu.">
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click on the little “i” button on the top right corner of the page again to see the dropdown menu.">
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select “Reload”.">
  </Step>
</Steps>
